How to play
Glowbloom in a nutshell
👀Watch the glowbuds light up, then tap them back in the same order.
⏱️When it's your turn you have at least 10 seconds to finish the pattern.
🥀A wrong tap or running out of time costs 1 Bud. You start with 4 Buds.
✨Clearing a pattern earns Light (your points). Longer patterns are worth more Light.
🔆Radiance is your streak multiplier. Each pattern you clear without a mistake raises it (up to x3) and multiplies the Light you earn. A wrong tap or a lost Bud resets it to x1.
🌷Out of Buds? Buy a Bud with Light (level × 10), up to 2 per run.
🎵Pick Sargam (Sa Re Ga…), Beeps, or Mute for tap sounds.
🏆Climb the Leaderboard — players tied on level & Light share a rank.
